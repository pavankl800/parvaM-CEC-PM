Timeless Poems in English That Shaped Literature 1. “The Road Not Taken” by Robert Frost When it comes to poems in English Robert Frost’s poem “The Road Not Taken” is a beloved classic that tackles the issue of choice and its impact on one’s life path. Frost’s vibrant images and thoughtful tone express the essence of decision-making and uniqueness. This timeless work, originally published in 1916, continues to resonate with readers due to its universal relevance and intellectual depth.
